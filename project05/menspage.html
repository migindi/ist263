College Age Men Nutrition (18-25)


Example of Nutrition Requirements

Grains:
• ½ cup Honey Nut Cherios
• ½ cup Whole Grain Pasta

Vegetables:
• ½ cup Pasta Sauce
• ½ green beans
• 1 cup broccoli
• 12 baby carrots

Fruits:
• 1 cup orange juice
• 1 apple

Proteins:
• 2 tbsp Peanut Butter
• 2 eggs
• 1 small chicken breast

Dairy:
• 1 cup pudding
• 1 cup parmesan cheese
• 1 cup skim milk



Follow this example of nutritional requirements to maintain a healthy diet!
